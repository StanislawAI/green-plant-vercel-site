Safety Inwestycji

1. Hermetic Process

Slurry goes through pipes directly to closed tanks. Lack of open storage strongly limits the odor source.

2. Negative Pressure Hall

Solid substrates are received in a hall with forced negative pressure. When the gate opens, air is sucked into the interior, rather than being pushed out.

3. Bio-Filtry Korzeniowe

Exhaust air passes through water scrubbers and organic beds, where natural microorganisms neutralize up to ~99% of compounds odor-forming.

Questions from Farmers

Most common questions, that we hear during visits at farms i local meetings.

"How much land will I lose?"

The plant occupies ~0.7 ha (approx. 0.4% at 180 ha). Rest of the fields work normally — and even better thanks to digestate.

"Do I need to have 200 cows?"

No. Feedstock balance and logistics are what counts. We can work on a mix from several farms.

"What if Green Plant goes bankrupt?"

SPV is a separate entity. Plant, contracts and insurance All-Risk stay with you. Shares are transferable.

"What about my direct payments?"

No changes. CAP payments are calculated per crop. The biogas plant occupies only 0.7 ha of built-up land.

"How much time will it take daily?"

Usually about 1 hour for feedstock logistics. Monitoring, service, and settlements are handled by Green Plant.

"Can I exit the contract?"

Tak. The SPV agreement provides clauses for the disposal of shares — you can sell them or hand them over to children as ready assets.
